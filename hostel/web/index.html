Aryan Hostel is one of the best, secure & affordable Boys hostel in Dehradun for university, college & academy students. It provides the best home like facilities in accommodation, food, security, environment and even transport facility. As being in the middle of the city, it is nearest to many educational institutes as well as business locations likeLaw Collge, BeeHive College, Uttarakhand Technical University, Selakui Industrial Area, University of Petroleum & Energy Studies (UPES), Forest Research Institute, Indian Military Academy, Clock Tower, Railway Station, etc; all the locations are at a drive of 1 to max 50 mins.
